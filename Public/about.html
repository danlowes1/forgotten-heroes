About Forgotten Legends

Our Mission

Forgotten Legends exists to shine a light on the remarkable individuals who made significant contributions to our world but never received the recognition they deserved. In an era of fleeting fame and algorithm-driven attention, we preserve the stories that truly matter.

We believe history isn't just made by the names in textbooks - it's shaped by countless unsung heroes whose legacies deserve to be remembered.

How This Project Began

The idea for Forgotten Legends came when our founder tried to research Jimmy Glass - the goalkeeper whose last-minute goal saved Carlisle United in 1999. Despite this being one of football's most dramatic moments, comprehensive information was surprisingly hard to find.

This discovery led to a simple question: "How many other extraordinary stories are being lost to time?" Our website is the answer to that question.

Our Core Values

Historical Accuracy

We rigorously fact-check every story, working with historians and primary sources.

Inclusive Perspective

We actively seek stories across gender, racial, and cultural lines that traditional history overlooked.

Educational Focus

Our content is designed to inspire learning and curiosity about the past.

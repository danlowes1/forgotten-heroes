Legends is a word that's lost its meaning.

Shame on us for letting it become ordinary. Here are the real legends - the ones we've forgotten.

Jimmy Glass

The goalkeeper who scored a last-minute goal to save Carlisle United from relegation in one of football's most dramatic moments.

Oscar Micheaux

The first major African-American filmmaker, producing and directing over 44 feature films between 1919 and 1948, challenging stereotypes when Black faces were rarely seen on screen.

Rosalind Franklin

The chemist whose X-ray diffraction images were critical to discovering DNA's structure, yet her contributions were largely unrecognized during her lifetime.

Henrietta Lacks

Her cancer cells were taken without consent and became one of medicine's most important tools, revolutionizing research while her family remained unaware for decades.

Carol Kenyon

British Session Singer • Heaven 17 Collaborator • 1980s Icon

Carol Kenyon is one of the most recognisable yet underrated voices of the 1980s British music scene. As a sought-after session singer, she worked with numerous iconic bands but is best known for her unforgettable performance on Heaven 17's classic hit "Temptation".

Despite her powerful vocals and significant contributions to the music of the era, Kenyon's name remains largely unknown to the general public, making her a true "forgotten hero" of British music.

Beyond her work with Heaven 17, Kenyon has sung with acts like Tears for Fears, Pink Floyd, and Mike Oldfield, showcasing her incredible versatility as a vocalist.
